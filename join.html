PAIR call for BS/MS research assistants

We are looking for students to work on several amazing projects that involve

  • Deep reinforcement and imitation learning on physical robots.
  • Visual representation learning for physical interaction.
  • Deep learning-based methods for planning & control.
  • Simulation to real-world policy transfer.
  • Large-scale robotic teleoperation for robotic manipulation tasks.

The primary qualification is interest in doing research, and if you have that we encourage you to apply even if you have not had prior research experience or have limited experience with AI. We strive to be a diverse and inclusive group that is open to students from all backgrounds.

Requirements
  • At least 10 hours per week of expected commitment.
  • Python/C++ programming
  • Experience in a subset of Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Reinforcement Learning, or Robotics
More details
  • Junior and 1st year MS preferred.
  • Graduating Seniors and MS students considered on a case by case basis.
  • Additional bonus points for: more in-depth coursework (Convex Optimization, Advanced Reinforcement Learning, Graphical Models, Advanced Robotics, Manipulation), familiarity with deep learning frameworks such as TF/PyTorch, and experience with using simulators such as MuJoCo, cloud infrastructure, or mobile app development
